Author INFO

He once would like to be a white-hat (web pentester) years ago. In one of his projects, he found it’s inevitable to get the hang of low-level basics (assembly, binary, runtime etc.) if he wants to learn the state-of-the-art techs or lanch a cutting-edge research himself. At that moment, he decided to dive in to the world of binary, where he found the interests of algo, cryptography and security.

Nowadays, his main interests are in the packing N unpacking techniques at the binaries on Windows N Linux (Aim to protect the integrity and confidentiality of the binary). He plans to build an open-source binary packing/unpacking tool with some freaky self-developed packing mechanism, which provide different secure-level protection, user can choose the needed mechanism to fullfill their requirements.

The tool is still under development, if you have any small and sweet ideas (would be better if you already have the algos in mind) about the bluring or encrypting to the binary, feel free to contact me if you want to add it to the tool.
